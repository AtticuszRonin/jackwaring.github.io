My Vision
This Is Our Moment.
AI is reshaping every industry. Goldman Sachs predicts over 300 million jobs will be impacted by AI. But we don't know how exactly. My vision is simple: equip every LSE student with the knowledge, tools and confidence to thrive in an AI-driven world.
Here's Our Agenda.

Get Ahead. Stay Ahead.

Speakers at the intersection of AI, geopolitics, finance, consulting, government and law. Whether you want to go down these career paths, AI is changing every path. Time to come understand yours.

Walk In Curious. Walk Out Capable.

A structured weekly workshop programme open to all levels. Each session focuses on a different AI tool from Claude Code to OpenClaw. By the end, you won't just know what AI can do. You'll know how to use it.

The Big Questions Start Here.

The debates shaping our world: Who controls AI? Can democracy survive AI? How can AI be used ethically? These questions deserve more than headlines. In partnership with societies across LSE, we bring these questions into the academic arena where they belong.

Beyond The Noise.

A student-written digest cutting through the hype on the developments that actually matter. Giving passionate students the opportunity to research the economics, ethics, governance, geopolitics and policy of AI. Giving students the tools to explore the evolving world of AI, the opportunity to express their voice on the issues that matter, and the insight to engage with the debates defining our generation.
